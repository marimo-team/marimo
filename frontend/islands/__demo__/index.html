LaTeX

You can embed LaTeX in Markdown. For example, 
mo.md(r'$f : \mathbf{R}     o \mathbf{R}$')
renders ||(f : \mathbf{R} o \mathbf{R}||) , while 
mo.md(
    r'''
    \[
    f: \mathbf{R}   o \mathbf{R}
    \]
    '''
)
